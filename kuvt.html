Kutztown University Virtual Tour

The KUAR ( Kutztown University Augmented Reality ) application is an Android app build from the ground up by a team of three computer science students at Kutztown University, with the help of two communication design students. This Android application is designed to allow a prospective student to tour the campus and retrieve information about the various buildings on the academic portion of the Kutztown campus. The app has two main functions, the first being an augmented reality view through an Android device. When the device's camera is aimed at an academic building, information will appear onscreen. The second function is an interactive map of campus, where buildings can be selected and information displayed. The user's location is also displayed on the map so they can effectively complete a self guided tour of campus.
